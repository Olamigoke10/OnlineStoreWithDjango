History

  • 2010: R-Foods opens its doors in Lagos, Nigeria, offering a unique dining experience that blends traditional Nigerian cuisine with international flavors.

  • 2012: R-Foods introduces its signature dish, the "Blissful Jollof", which becomes an instant hit with customers.

  • 2014: The restaurant launches its catering service, providing delicious meals for events and parties across the city.




  • 2015: R-Foods wins the "Best Restaurant in Lagos" award at the Nigerian Food Awards.

  • 2016: The restaurant expands its menu to include vegan and gluten-free options, catering to the growing demand for healthy eating.

  • 2017: R-Foods celebrates its 7th anniversary with a grand reopening, unveiling a newly renovated dining area and a new menu featuring contemporary Nigerian cuisine.
